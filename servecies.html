- Welcome to our center:

We are a comprehensive medical center that offers a wide range of healthcare services, staffed by highly experienced and specialized doctors. Our team includes physicians from various specialties such as pediatrics, general surgery, and dentistry.

- Our medical services include:

Pediatrics:

We provide comprehensive care for children, including care for premature and newborn infants. We offer all necessary vaccinations for children at various stages of development, thorough medical examinations,

General Surgery:

We offer early medical diagnosis for conditions that may require surgical intervention. Our center performs all types of emergency treatments and surgeries. We are contracted with a wide network of hospitals and clinics to ensure the best possible outcomes. We also carry out all necessary emergency procedures.

Dentistry:

Our dental services include a wide range of treatments such as endodontic (root canal) therapy, periodontal care, and oral surgeries, including tooth extractions. We also provide high-quality fillings, including veneers and light-cured fillings, in addition to professional teeth cleaning and whitening using the latest equipment and top-grade materials.
